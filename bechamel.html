Calories: 60

Servings: 4

Prep Time: 15 mins

Steps

  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour

Steps

  • Melt butter in pan
  • Add flour to melted butter and mix until paste like
  • Add milk to paste and stir on low heat until thick and creamy
  • Enjoy!
