Calories:200

Servings: 2

Prep Time: 35 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 Eggs
  • Salt

Steps

  • Add flour to a bowl with a pinch of salt and make a well
  • Add egg into well and stir intil combined
  • kneed dough until bouncy but not sticky
  • wrap and place dough in fridge to proof for 30 minutes
  • Roll out dough and put through pasta cutter
  • place cut pasta in boiling water and cook until firm but chewy
  • Enjoy!
