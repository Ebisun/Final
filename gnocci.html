Calories: 300

Servings: 4

Prep Time: 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 6 potatoes
  • Flour
  • 1 Egg

Steps

  • Boil potatoes
  • Peel boiled potatoes and mash
  • Add egg to mashed potatoes
  • Slowly incorporate flour into potatoes while kneading until doughy but not sticky.
  • Roll out dough and cut into bite sized pieces
  • place pieces into boiling water and cook until they float back up
  • Strain and Enjoy!
