Calories: 80

Servings: 6

Prep Time: 30 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 2 cups chopped fruit (Strawberries)
  • 1 tsp Lemon Juice

Steps

  • Add fruit to saucepan with sugar
  • cook on medium heat until fruit is soft
  • Add lemon juice
  • Mash soft fruit and strain with fine strainer
  • Enjoy!
